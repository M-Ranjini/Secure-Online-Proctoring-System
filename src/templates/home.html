Process

How it works.

Four steps from registration to result — with AI active at every stage.

1
Register & Enrol
Students sign up and complete face biometric registration. Their facial encoding is securely stored for identity verification.
2
Pre-Exam Check
Camera, network, and biometric readiness are verified. Students review the compliance checklist before launching.
3
Live Proctored Exam
AI monitors gaze, face identity, device presence, and behaviour throughout. Violations are logged with timestamped screenshots.
4
Results & Report
Scores are published instantly. Admins receive a full violation timeline. Students see their result and any flagged incidents.
